Coke Zero: Fan Combine

Coca-Cola wanted to connect to the fiercely loyal and boisterous fans of college football, an audience comprised of their target demographic. If they could increase fan engagement and visit length to their events taking place during games nationwide, they could separate themselves from their competitors and establish a better connection between soda drinkers and their brand.
