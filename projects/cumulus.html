Cumulus Media: Digital Realign

Cumulus wanted to re-invent radio, but in order to understand and engage their emerging online fan base, they had to have a solid understanding of user experience and web-based trends. They needed expert insight to serve as the foundation for a shift in their digital marketing efforts and a system to help them manage audience engagement across all of their radio stations.

I started from the ground up, beginning with a digital audit that revealed emerging trends such as DJ-driven streaming services that their fans were clamoring for. Analytics confirmed that radio is largely driven by local audiences, so we created a socially driven CMS platform that allowed stations and shows to curate content distributed to their Facebook pages with a consistent voice.
