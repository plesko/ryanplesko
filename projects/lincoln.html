Lincoln Intelligence Project

Lincoln had their strategy figured out and needed it executed quickly: Four touch-based tablet games designed to test participants recognition, memory, aptitude and intuition, all while supporting the brand’s new image.

With a ridiculously short deadline, the project required almost impossibly efficient management. By asking the right questions I was able to design and create the games that went into service with the precision Lincoln required. The games used Lincoln’s new models’ features and competing vehicle comparisons to test participants at each event, who were then able to see their performance and ranking online as well as share their philanthropic involvement.
