Meta: Making Smart the New Sexy

Everyone knows the Fossil brand, but Meta, their smartwatch spin-off, struggled to establish a foothold in the burgeoning smartwatch market. They needed something that set them apart from the function-only alternatives stealing their web traffic. They needed to create a desire for their luxury product. Bottom line? They needed conversions.

Meta’s business model drives sales almost exclusively through the Internet, so we needed to do more than just revamp their image. I integrated advanced analytics into their marketing processes to create a brand driven by hard data with a feel that targeted their style-conscious, discerning clientele. The goal was to combine dry analysis of web traffic with a thorough understanding of nuanced online conversation. Starting with a website redesign and moving organically through a brand new social media strategy, we established a brand name with a consistent value proposition: wearable tech is the new sexy.
